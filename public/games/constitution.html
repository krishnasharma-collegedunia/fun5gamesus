How to Play ▾
  • An article card appears at the top.
  • Read the article number and title carefully.
  • Press & drag the card down to the correct Part.
  • Drop it on the right Part: card snaps in, you score +10.
  • Wrong drop: card shakes, the correct Part flashes briefly. -5 points.
  • Build a streak for bonus points (+2 per consecutive correct).
