How to Playv
  • 5 Prime Ministers on the LEFT, 5 facts on the RIGHT.
  • Tap a PM card, then tap the matching fact to draw a connection line.
  • Tap a connected card again to break and re-draw.
  • Connect all 5 pairs, then SUBMIT to reveal answers.
  • Green line = correct, Red line = wrong. Read explanations.
  • Play 5 rounds = 25 connections total.
  • +20 per correct, +time bonus + streak bonus (3 in a row).
  • Be quick: timer drains every round.
