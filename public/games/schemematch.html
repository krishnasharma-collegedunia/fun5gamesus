HOW TO
PLAY

Goal. Match every NAME card to its DESCRIPTION card.

Tap a face-down card to flip it, then tap a second card.

If they form a pair, both stay revealed and a brief explanation appears for a moment.

If they do not match, both flip back after one second and a mistake is counted.

Categories. Choose All for variety, or pick a focus area: Welfare, Rural, Urban, Banking, Health, Education, Agriculture, Women or Digital.

Difficulty. Easy = 8 pairs (4×4), Medium = 12 pairs (4×6), Hard = 16 pairs (4×8).

Par time. Roughly half your best time + 30 seconds. Beat par for a 2-star result.

Best time is saved separately for each Category + Difficulty combination on this device.

Stars. 1 star to win, 2 to beat par, 3 for a brand-new best.

UPSC tip. The popup after a match shows the full scheme name, launch year and one-line description. Use it as a quick revision aid for prelims and mains.

Verified. All 68 schemes are sourced from india.gov.in, pib.gov.in and official ministry portals.
