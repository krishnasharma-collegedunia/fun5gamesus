Who We Are

Prepp Games is the dedicated brain games and arcade section of Prepp.in — India's trusted online platform for government exam preparation. We've curated 29+ free HTML5 games designed to sharpen the cognitive skills that matter most for competitive exams: speed math, logical reasoning, English vocabulary, general knowledge, and working memory.

Our Mission

Government exam preparation is intense. Aspirants spend hours grinding through mock tests, current affairs, and concept-heavy notes. Prepp Games exists to make those study breaks productive — instead of scrolling social media, play a 60-second math sprint, solve a Sudoku, or tackle a UPSC-grade Constitution Quest.

Every game is engineered around a real exam skill. Quick Math 60 builds Bank PO calculation speed. Word Wise tackles SSC vocabulary. Constitution Quest reinforces UPSC Polity. India Map Master drills geography. Whether you're prepping for IAS, SSC CGL, IBPS PO, or RRB NTPC — there's a game for the section you're weakest in.

What We Offer

  • 13 Brain & Skills games — Quick Math, Sudoku Pro, GK Blitz, Number Ninja, Code Cracker, Memory Master, Word Groups, Word Wise, India Map, Mini Crossword, True or False, Odd One Out, DI Drill
  • 4 UPSC Special games — Constitution Quest, Timeline Rush, Scheme Match, PM Quest (interactive drag-drop and matching, not boring quizzes)
  • 12 Arcade classics — Fruit Slice, Snake.Go, Pixel Flap, 2048 Merge, Block Drop, Bubble Shooter, and more — for those guilt-free study breaks
  • 2,000+ verified questions across all GK, vocabulary, and quiz games — sourced from GKToday, Testbook, Wikipedia, PIB, and other trusted references
  • 100% browser-based — no signups, no payments, no data harvesting. Just open and play.

Who We Serve

Our games are tailored for aspirants of major Indian competitive exams:

  • UPSC Civil Services (IAS, IPS, IFS) — Polity, History, Geography, Schemes
  • SSC (CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD) — Quant, Reasoning, English, GK
  • Banking (IBPS PO/Clerk, SBI PO/Clerk, RBI Grade B) — Speed math, DI, English
  • Railway (RRB NTPC, Group D, ALP, RPF) — General Awareness, Math, Reasoning
  • Defense (NDA, CDS, AFCAT) — General Knowledge, English, Math
  • Teaching (CTET, UGC NET) — Reasoning, English, Child Development
