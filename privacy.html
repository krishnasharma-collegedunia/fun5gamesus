Privacy Policy

How Fun5Games collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Overview

3.14 Digital is the manager, moderator, and operator of the Fun5Games website (fun5games.us) and the games section on Prepp.in. This policy applies exclusively to Fun5Games and Prepp.in, and not to any linked external websites.

By using Fun5Games, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Users Under 13 Years of Age

Fun5Games is not designed for users below 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Any such data discovered will be promptly deleted from our systems.

3. Cookies and Tracking

The site collects non-personal identifiable information including:

  • IP addresses
  • Cookies and local storage data
  • Search history within the platform
  • Game interaction data (which games you play, scores, time spent)
  • Educational interests and exam preferences
  • Timestamps and domain referrer data

A conversion pixel may track user behavior on lead-collection forms as part of standard operations. We use cookies to remember your high scores, game preferences, and improve your experience across visits.

4. Information Collection Methods

Game Play (No Login Required)

Game scores, streaks, and preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage — this data never leaves your device unless you choose to share scores via the in-game share feature.

Contact Forms (on Prepp.in)

If you contact us through Prepp.in forms, we may gather your name, email, phone number, educational qualifications, and exam interests. By submitting these forms, you consent to receive SMS and emails containing information about exams and courses you're interested in.

Testimonials and Reviews

If you submit a testimonial or game review, you grant us permission for public display and sharing with educational service providers.

Forums and Comments

"Views posted on forums and our comment section are sole property of Prepp.in/Fun5Games" and may be moderated, edited, or removed at our discretion.

5. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalize your gaming experience and recommend relevant games
  • Send customized newsletters with exam updates (with unsubscribe options)
  • Request feedback on new game features
  • Optimize search results and content delivery based on your interests
  • Display location-based content where relevant
  • Serve relevant advertising through third-party ad networks

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdSense / Vertoz — for serving advertisements (uses cookies)
  • Google Analytics — for usage analytics (anonymized)
  • CDN providers — for fast content delivery

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data handling.

7. Calendar Authentication (on Prepp.in app)

When using calendar features on Prepp.in, we may request access for appointment reminders and schedule syncing. Data accessed includes event titles, times, dates, locations, and attendee information. This data is not shared with third parties and remains within the Prepp.in application.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

If you have a Prepp.in account, you can permanently delete it through app settings after logging in. Local game data (high scores, streaks) on Fun5Games can be cleared anytime by clearing your browser's localStorage / site data.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Decline cookies via browser settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
